Our History

Jensen Huang, Chris Malachowsky, and Curtis Priem founded NVIDIA in 1993. The company invented the GPU in 1999, sparking the growth of the PC gaming market and redefining modern computer graphics.

The Era of AI

In 2012, researchers used NVIDIA GPUs to ignite the modern AI big bang. Today, NVIDIA is the engine of the world's AI infrastructure.
